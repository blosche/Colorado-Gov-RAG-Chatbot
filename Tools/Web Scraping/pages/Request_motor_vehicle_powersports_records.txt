Auto Industry CORA Request
The Colorado Auto Industry Division provides public documents from its motor vehicle/powersports records. Document requests can usually be completed within three business days after receipt of request. Unusually large or complicated requests may require up to seven business days to complete.
Per CRS 24-72-205, the fees for public records are as follows:
Staff costs: $30/hr. after the first hour
Photocopies: $0.25 per page
Media:
CD $0.50
DVD  $1.00
*Note: No fees are required for law enforcement and/or government agency inquires.

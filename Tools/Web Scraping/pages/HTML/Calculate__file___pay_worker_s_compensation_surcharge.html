Workers' Compensation Surcharge Filing & Payment for Commercial Carriers and Self-Insurers

Welcome to the Colorado Division of Workers' Compensation Surcharge web application. This web application is only for workers' compensation commercial carriers and self-insured employers who have been authorized by special permit to self-insure their workers’ compensation liabilities.

If you are unsure whether you are a commercial carrier or self-insured employer, please email cdle_revenue_assessment@state.co.us.

Online filing for the reporting period January 1, 2024 through June 30, 2024 will be available beginning July 1, 2024. The total Surcharge Rates for this reporting period is: 1.40 for Self Insurers and 1.43 for Carriers.

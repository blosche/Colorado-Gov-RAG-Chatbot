We offer resources to help you understand the regulations and how they apply to you, including workshops and other training designed to clarify the regulations and provide an open forum for questions.

2024 Colorado Hazardous Waste Training: Overview of Regulations

We are hosting a one-day in person training, and a one-day virtual training to provide a refresher of Colorado’s hazardous waste regulations. This training provides an overview of the main hazardous waste topics, information on what to expect on an inspection, and allows generators to ask questions of division staff.

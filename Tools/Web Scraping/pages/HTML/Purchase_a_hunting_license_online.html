Things to know when buying online or by phone 

  • The 2024 annual license year runs 3/1/2024 - 3/31/2025 (13 months). 

  • Over-the-counter unlimited spring turkey licenses go on sale March 1, 2024 and annual mountain lion licenses March 25, 2024.

  • Customers must complete all requirements for Hunter Education before they apply for or buy a hunting license or before they apply for a preference point.

  • You must have your physical license to hunt any animal that requires a carcass tag such as big game and turkey, the TAN does not apply. Items sent via standard shipping may take 30 days to arrive from your order date.

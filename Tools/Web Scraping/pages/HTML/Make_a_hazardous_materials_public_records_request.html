Search tips and tricks - text instructions
 
Records containing confidential information or that are exempt from public release under open records laws may not be displayed. If there is a specific record you believe is missing from a search result, please send a request to cdphe_cora_hmwmd@state.co.us and provide the document type you are seeking.

Submit your request online

Types of records

HMWMD records include solid waste, hazardous waste, radiation management, X-ray, and environmental cleanup.

Reviewing files

  • We'll respond to all requests within three business days.
  • You must have an appointment to review files. Hours available are Monday through Friday, 9:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m. and 1:00 p.m. to 4:00 p.m. Onsite file reviews may be reduced or eliminated due to COVID-19 restrictions.
  • Large scanning requests will be performed by the state’s contracted vendor. Fees will apply.
  • Per department policy, some requests will be subject to fees.

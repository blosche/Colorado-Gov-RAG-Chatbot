Notice of Rulemaking
As part of CDPHE's regulatory plan, a rule review of the Vital Statistics rule, 5 CCR 1006-1 was due. After conducting the rule review, the program has proposed changes to the rule, and seeking your feedback. Please click the top link below to access the rule packet, which contains information necessary to understand the scope and purpose behind the rule changes, as well as a marked up version of the rule (at the end of the packet). To submit feedback, please click the link below to access the stakeholder feedback form:
